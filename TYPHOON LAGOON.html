Typhoon Lagoon

Typhoon Lagoon provides a perfect combination of thrills, relaxation, and tropical charm. Whether you're looking to ride exhilarating water slides, bask in the sun on a sandy beach, or simply enjoy a day of aquatic adventures, Typhoon Lagoon offers a unique and enjoyable experience for visitors of all ages.

Top 3 Attractions

Crush 'n' Gusher

  • Height requirement: 48 inches or taller
  • Enter an abandoned fruit-processing plant and prepare for a thrilling “water coaster” ride down a 400-foot-long tube slide. Slide down sudden drops and snake around sharp turns through darkened and open sections. Propelled by powerful water jets, your raft takes you on a wild ride through uphill climbs—and surprise after surprise.

Miss Adventure Falls

  • Height requirement: none
  • From your 4-person raft, feel a rush of excitement with family and friends as an aquatic conveyor belt chutes you into action. Then, hold on tight as you spy precious artifacts collected from all around the globe.

Typhoon Lagoon Surf Pool

  • Height requirement: none
  • Wade into the warm water where gentle waves lap the white sandy beach. Swim out and listen for the thunderous “sonic boom” and the squeals of delighted swimmers as little ripples become big kahunas. Keep watch and feel the excitement build as a cresting wave heads your way—and get ready to paddle!
