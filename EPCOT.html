Epcot

Epcot offers a unique blend of entertainment, education, and cultural exploration, making it a park that appeals to a wide range of visitors. Whether you're interested in technological innovation, international culture, or simply enjoying a day of fun and discovery, EPCOT has something for everyone.

Top 3 Attractions

Guardian of the Galaxy: Cosmic Rewind

  • Height requirement: 42 inches or taller
  • The ride takes guests on a journey through the cosmos with Marvel’s oddball team, Guardians of the Galaxy. This indoor roller coaster is a pure thrill and boasts one of the most innovative ride technologies seen to date. The ride vehicle is a unique coaster car that rotates 360 degrees as you travel forward and backward along the coaster track.

Remy's Ratatouille Adventure

  • Height requirement:none
  • In this 4D ride experience, join Chef Remy on a daring culinary caper that will captivate all your senses as you zip, dash and scurry through the bustling kitchen, dining room and walls of Gusteau’s famous Paris restaurant.

Gran Fiesta Tour Starring The Three Caballeros

  • Height requirement:none
  • Cruise down the Rio Grande on a dark boat ride past some of the most famous sights and cities in Mexico.
