Blizzard Beach

Blizzard Beach offers a fun and immersive escape from the Florida heat, where guests can experience the thrill of water slides and attractions while surrounded by the whimsy of a wintry ski resort. It's a perfect destination for families and water park enthusiasts looking for a unique and refreshing experience within the Walt Disney World Resort.

Top 3 Attractions

Summit Plummet

  • Height requirement: 48 inches or taller
  • Plunge 12 stories almost straight down, rocketing through a darkened tunnel and into a massive spray of whitewater after a 360-foot-long, high-speed descent.

Runoff Rapids

  • Height requirement: none
  • Experience 3 times the thrills as you twist and turn your way 600 feet down the mountain. On the center slide, you’ll launch on a dark, fully enclosed trip to the bottom with just pinpricks of light peeking through. On the 2 outer slides, you’ll zip over, around and under the snaking coils of the center slide.

Toboggan Racers

  • Height requirement: none
  • Grab your mat, take your mark and zip headfirst on your belly to the bottom of Mount Gushmore on a 250-foot-long “toboggan” race that features 3 exhilarating dips and hills.
