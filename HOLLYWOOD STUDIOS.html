Hollywood Studios offers an immersive and entertaining experience that allows visitors to step into the world of showbiz and experience the magic of movies and television. Whether you're a fan of classic films or modern blockbusters, there's something for everyone to enjoy at this dynamic theme park.

Top 3 Attractions

Slinky Dog Dash

  • Height requirement: 38 inches or taller
  • Andy’s assembled his Mega Coaster Play Kit—and Slinky Dog is taking you on a wild ride. Slinky Dog’s springy coils stretch to the limit as you bend around curves, zoom up hills and drop back down.

Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge

  • Height requirement: 38 inches or taller
  • Live your Star Wars Adventure in a Galaxy Far, Far Away

Indiana Jones Epic Stunt Spectacular

  • Height requirement: none
  • Watch live as the daring duo dodges deadly traps, battles bad guys, leaps from tall buildings and makes thrilling escapes straight out of Raiders of the Lost Ark. The edge-of-your-seat suspense ends with a dynamite finale!
