Animal Kingdom

Animal Kingdom is not only a place for entertainment but also a hub for environmental education and wildlife conservation, fostering a deep appreciation for the natural world and its inhabitants while maintaining the magic and storytelling for which Disney is renowned. It's a place where adventure, exploration, and imagination come together in a truly unforgettable way.

Top 3 Attractions

Kilimanjaro Safaris

  • Height requirement: none
  • Explore the Harambe Wildlife Reserve, home to over 30 species living in 110 acres of picturesque open plains, shady forest landscapes and rocky wetlands. Your rugged safari vehicle is driven by an expert guide, who helps point out animals and shares fascinating wildlife facts during this extraordinary 18-minute expedition.

Guardian of the Galaxy: Cosmic Rewind

  • Height requirement: 44 inches or taller
  • After a steep ascent, your train picks up speed and races into the darkness. Without warning, a broken and twisted track appears in front of you. Brace yourself as you plummet backward into total darkness. Inside the cavern, the mythical tale comes true as you see the shadow of a massive, growling beast! Race to escape the cursed mountain—before the Yeti claims another expedition.

Kali River Rapids

  • Height requirement: 38 inches or taller
  • Skim across an erupting geyser, drift below a canopy of lush vegetation and be whisked along fast-moving rapids. Graze gushing waterfalls and bedrock amid the raging current. Then, as the harmony of nature is disrupted, you’ll plummet down a dramatic 20-foot slope!
